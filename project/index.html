Short Description

A single-player action role-playing game called Black Myth: Wukong is based on the Journey to the West.

Learn More

Introduction

The game uses the narrative of Wu Cheng'en's Journey to the West, one of the four great novels of China, as a preface to tell the story of Sun Wukong, who abandoned his position as a Buddha and set off the Heavenly Court's conquest of him once more following the search for the scriptures. In order to discover the truth behind the historical legend, players will assume the character of a "Heavenly Ordained Man" and set off on a perilous and unexpected journey to the West.

Why Play Black Myth: Wukong?

Exceptional graphic performance

Black Myth: Wukong employs cutting-edge graphics technology to deliver incredibly delicate and exquisite game graphics, achieving a very high degree of excellence in character modeling, environmental details, and light and shadow effects.

Sophisticated Fighting System

The fighting system in the game has a lot of depth. Gamers will assume control of the Monkey King, who can battle against a variety of demons and creatures using a broad range of spells and fighting maneuvers. Players can select and employ various magical items and weaponry throughout the game.

Exploration and problem Solving

The game has a lot of exploration and problem solving components in addition to its dramatic fights. The large game environment must be explored by players in order to find hidden treasures and secrets.
