Game Meaning and Reflection

Black Myth: Wukong is China's first purely domestic 3A game masterpiece, shocking the whole industry. This proves that China also has the ability to do this. Secondly, the global release of the game allows the culture of the game to be seen by the whole world. Data indicates that foreign players are learning about Wukong's background for a better gaming experience. The search volume of Journey to the West, one of China's four great masterpieces, has increased dozens of times on Google.
