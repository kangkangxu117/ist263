About Black Myth: Wukong

Black Myth: Wukong is a single-player action role-playing game based on Journey to the West. It tells the story of Sun Wukong, the Monkey King, who leaves his position as Buddha and embarks on a perilous journey to discover the truth behind an ancient legend.

Why Play Black Myth: Wukong?

  • Exceptional Graphic Performance: Cutting-edge graphics technology brings detailed character models, environments, and light effects to life.
  • Sophisticated Fighting System: Control the Monkey King with a wide range of spells and fighting maneuvers.
  • Transformation Abilities: Transform into various forms with unique abilities for different combat scenarios.
  • Exploration and Problem Solving: Discover hidden treasures and secrets in a vast game environment.
  • Cultural Depth: Rich cultural elements reflecting traditional Chinese philosophy.
